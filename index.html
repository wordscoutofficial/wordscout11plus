Who we are

About WordScout11Plus

WordScout11Plus and SpellScout11Plus are fun, vibrant learning companions designed to boost your child's confidence and performance in 11+ exams. With colourful visuals, interactive quizzes, and thoughtfully chosen vocabulary, our books turn practice into play.

📚

Curriculum-aligned vocabulary

Every word is carefully selected to match what actually appears in GL, CEM and independent school 11+ papers.

🎯

Built for daily practice

Short, focused sessions designed around the science of spaced repetition — 10 minutes a day beats 2 hours once a week.

🧠

Active recall built in

Quizzes, definitions, and example sentences are structured so children test themselves — the most effective study method there is.

🌈

Fun and engaging design

Colourful, approachable visuals that children actually want to open — not another dry workbook.
